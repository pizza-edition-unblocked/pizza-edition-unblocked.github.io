How to Play Zombie Derby: Pixel Survival

In Zombie Derby: Pixel Survival, you drive and shoot! Use keys to race a car and blast zombies. Survive levels to win. Can you escape in this action game?

  • WASD: Drive car.
  • Mouse Click: Shoot zombies.
  • R: Restart level.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Drive, shoot, and win in this super fun action game! Play now on CrazyGames!

Description of Zombie Derby: Pixel Survival

Zombie Derby: Pixel Survival is an awesome action game where you use WASD to drive a car and your mouse to shoot zombies in a pixel-art world. Upgrade your car with armor or guns using coins from levels. Race through zombie hordes to reach the finish. The game’s retro 2D graphics and spooky sounds make every drive a thrill. This action game is perfect for kids who love zombies and racing!

Kids love this game because it’s super exciting to smash zombies and speed away. The cool cars and tough levels keep you driving to win and be a zombie-slaying champ. Can you survive? Try Zombie Derby: Pixel Survival now and race into action game fun!
